About Glifico
Glifico is a platform that allows translation agencies to contact translators and interpreters from all over the world and translate documents into any language.
Our Vision
We want to create a brand new way to communicate and collaborate faster and easier without geographical limits, increasing job opportunities and efficiency of the usual workflow.
Our Mission
To create a dynamic and interactive environment that allows agencies to find the best translators or interpreters for a specific project using a data validation system based on ratings and feedback.

Here is what we think about the project

Why for agencies?

We are confident that the agencies have an important role because they link customers to freelance. They can guarantee quality of service to their customers and proper recognition of the freelance's professionalism.

Why should an interpreter or translator sign in?

Glifico is a new way for professionals to boost their work opportunities. They can get in touch with agencies throughout the world. On the other hand Glifico allows agencies to choose the right professionist looking at the rating or the comments of other agencies that have collaborated in the past or some example of translation or some video where the interpreters can present themselves. That’s important for a personal satisfaction of the translators and interpreters.

Why was Glifico born?

In this world it happens (always more frequently) that the linguistic industry has to face with customers who are interested in asking only for lower prices. Obviously it causes a problem for the quality of the service. Now professionals can offer their own skills for how much it worth.

At the same time, agencies can give information to the customers in a clearly, precise and fast way. You don’t need to negotiate! That’s annoying. In this way everything will be clear from the beginning and that makes Glifico faster.
