About Glifico
Glifico is a platform that allows translation agencies to contact translators and interpreters from all the world and translate documents into any languages.
Our Vision
We want to create a brand new way to communicate and collaborate faster and easier without geographical limits, increasing job opportunities and efficiency of the usual workflow.
Our Mission
To create a dynamic and interactive environment that allows allows agencies to find the best translators or interpreters for a specific project using a data validation system based on ratings and feedback.

Here is what we think about the project

Why for agencies?

We are confident that the agencies have an important role because they link customers to freelance. They can guarantee quality of service to their customers and proper recognition of the freelance's professionalism.

Why should an interpreter or translator sign up?

Glifico is a new way for professionals to boost their work opportunities. They can get in touch with agencies throughout the world. On the other hand Glifico allows agencies to choose the right professionist looking at the rating or the comments of other agencies that have collaborated in the past or some samples of translation or some videos where the interpreters can present themselves. That’s important for a personal satisfaction of the translators and interpreters.

Why was Glifico born?

In this world it happens (always more frequently) that the linguistic industry face customers who are only concerned about lower prices. However, it may reflect the quality of the service.Now translators and interpreters can offer their service for how much it’s worth..

Furthermore agencies can fulfill requests more clearly, precisely and quickly No more sqeezing rates. This way everything will be clear from the beginning and that makes Glifico more efficient
